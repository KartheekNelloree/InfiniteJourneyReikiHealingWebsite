Meet Your Guide

Dr. Priya Sharma

Reiki Grand Master | Energy Healer | Spiritual Teacher

Dr. Priya Sharma's journey into the world of Reiki began over a decade ago, born from a personal crisis that conventional medicine could not resolve. Battling chronic fatigue and emotional burnout, she discovered Reiki almost by chance — and her life was forever changed. Within weeks of her first attunement, she experienced a profound shift that reignited her passion for life and health.

Driven by this transformation, Dr. Priya dedicated herself to mastering every level of Usui Reiki, eventually traveling to Japan to train under renowned Grand Master Hiroshi Doi. She holds advanced certifications in Karuna Reiki, Crystal Healing, and Pranic Healing, and has completed extensive training in chakra therapy, sound healing, and mindfulness meditation.

Today, she channels her expertise and compassion into helping hundreds of students and clients find their path to wholeness. Her teaching style blends ancient wisdom with modern understanding, making Reiki accessible and deeply effective for students of all backgrounds.

Our Core Beliefs

Philosophy, Mission & Vision

Our Philosophy

We believe every human being carries within them an innate capacity for healing. Reiki is not something we give to you — it is something we help you remember. True healing emerges from alignment with universal life force energy that flows through all living things. We hold a sacred space for that remembering.

Our Mission

To make authentic Reiki healing accessible to everyone, regardless of background or belief system. We are committed to educating and empowering individuals with practical energy healing skills that transform their health, relationships, and spiritual growth — creating ripples of healing throughout their communities.

Our Vision

A world where energy healing is recognized as an essential component of holistic health care. We envision a global community of Reiki practitioners who carry the light of healing into hospitals, schools, workplaces, and homes — bridging ancient wisdom with modern wellness to create a healthier, more compassionate humanity.
