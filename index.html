What is Reiki Healing?

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique based on the principle that the therapist can channel energy into the patient by means of touch or distance, activating the natural healing processes of the patient's body and restoring physical, emotional, and mental well-being.

Derived from the Japanese words "Rei" (Universal Life) and "Ki" (Energy), Reiki is a holistic approach that addresses not just symptoms but the root causes of imbalance in the body and mind.

  • Reduces stress and anxiety, promoting deep relaxation and inner peace
  • Accelerates natural healing and supports immune system function
  • Balances chakras and removes energetic blockages for holistic wellness
